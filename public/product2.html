Product Name:

ETC Continuous Solar DRYER

Production Description:

Solar energy is harnessed through Evacuated Tube Collectors (ETC), which absorb sunlight and convert it into heat. This collected energy warms the air inside the dryer, and as the warm air circulates over the food items, it absorbs moisture and expels it from the chamber. Additionally, excess heat is stored in the heat exchanger, providing an extra drying time of up to 5 hours.

Key Features:

  • Eco-Friendly and Contamination Free Drying
